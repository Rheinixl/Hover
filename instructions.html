Instructions for Typing Test

Thank you for participating! Please read the following instructions carefully.

  1. Overview: You will complete two rounds of a typing task.
  2. Task: Copy the paragraph you see in the Text window into the Typing window.
  3. Round 1:
    • Switch between the Text and Typing windows using the tabs at the top.
    • Click Text to view the paragraph and Type to return to typing.
    • Click Start Round to begin.
  4. Round 2:
    • The Typing window will stay visible at all times.
    • Hold the Ctrl key to temporarily show the Text window over the Typing window.
    • Release the Ctrl key to return to typing.
    • Click Start Round to begin, and you will see a reminder before the timer starts.
  5. Goal: Complete the task as quickly and accurately as possible.
  6. Note: After both rounds, your results will automatically download as a CSV file.
